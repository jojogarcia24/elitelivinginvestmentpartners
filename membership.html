Membership

Participation is ownership.

A seat at Elite Living Investment Partners isn't a subscription. It's a partnership — you contribute, you participate, and you share in what the community builds together.

Two paths to a seat

Buy in — or earn it.

There's more than one way to the table. Both make you a partner.

Buy in

Secure your seat directly and step into the partnership from day one. Terms and eligibility are shared during the application process.

Earn your seat

Prefer to prove it through work? Earn your place with participation — "sweat equity" — by staying active and contributing to the network.

What participation means

Keep your seat at the table.

Membership is active, not passive. The partners who show up are the partners who share in what gets built.

Stay active

Remain engaged with the community period over period.

Contribute & market

Bring your effort, your voice, and your network to the table.

Bring opportunities

Introduce opportunities and collaborate across the verticals.

The principle

"Members who actively participate each period share in what the partnership produces — distributed equally among qualifying partners."

Full participation requirements and membership terms are provided during the application process.
